My name is Jackson Spencer and I am a computer science Major at BYU-Idaho. I used to go to school for Piano Performance, but decided to pursue music as a hobby. I have been playing the piano since I was young and eventually I was enrolled in piano lessons when I was 8.

Piano is a passion of mine and I enjoy playing for others and teaching others how to play. I believe the piano is a gift that will only grow with the right people teaching piano lessons to the next generation. I want to be the reason that someone becomes inspired to play more, or at least give every child the opportunity to enjoy playing the piano.

Music in general has always been a passion to me, and I enjoy sharing my talents with others. If there is a campfire and a guitar I'll try to play around as you can see in the picture.
